# Learnings — map-competitive-arena

Captured patterns from running this skill. New learnings get appended; routine review by `improve-skill`.

## v1 baseline (2026-05-18)

- Strategic-group analysis is uniquely prone to taxonomy-without-insight failure. The fix is to force a winner-archetype call per group — without it, a group profile is just a description.
- The most common drafter failure is picking correlated axes (price × quality), which produces a diagonal. The fix is the 3-test rubric in `references/strategic-groups.md` — applied at intake before plotting.
- Mobility barriers are routinely confused with entry barriers. The reference explicitly distinguishes; the validator does not test for this (hard to detect mechanically). Trust the drafter to read the reference.
- Arenas overlay is the highest-leverage piece when applicable. Mature-market overlays add little; Arena and Contested-mature overlays often surface the most strategically-actionable insight (which group will redefine the arena, which barriers are eroding).
- Helmer 7-Powers cross-mapping (in `winner-archetypes.md`) is the natural handoff to `assess-moat-sources`. Keep that linkage tight.
