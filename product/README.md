# Product

Skills for product managers, founders, and product-led teams who need AI agents to run structured feedback analysis and idea validation workflows.

## Skills

| Skill | Description | When to Use |
|---|---|---|
| [analyzing-feedback](analyzing-feedback/) | Parses raw customer feedback (app reviews, support tickets, interview transcripts) into categorized severity-ranked themes, scans the codebase to identify affected files and components, and proposes 3 structural edit options per theme (minimal/refactor/architectural) | User provides raw customer feedback and wants to know what to change in the code; translating customer complaints into file-level engineering proposals |
| [find-my-business](find-my-business/) | Guides a founder interactively from "no idea" through idea generation, rapid validation, and commitment to a business — synthesizing PG, YC, Lean Startup, Mom Test, JTBD, and gstack office-hours methodology | Finding a business to start, exploring startup ideas, getting unstuck on what to build, brainstorming business directions |

## When to use this domain

Use product skills when you need a structured process that bridges qualitative input — customer language, founder intuition — and concrete action. Analyzing-feedback connects raw feedback directly to code files, avoiding the usual translation loss. Find-my-business is an interactive conversation, not a one-shot answer — it interviews, generates, validates, and narrows toward conviction across multiple sessions. Neither skill is for writing PRDs or pitch decks.
