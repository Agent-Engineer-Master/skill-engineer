## P&L benchmarks by revenue cohort (A2X 2025)

| Cohort | Gross margin | Contribution margin | EBITDA% |
|---|---|---|---|
| Under $10M | 67.1% | 30.9% | 7.9% |
| $10M–$50M | 69.8% | 27.2% | 7.3% |
| Over $50M | 79.3% | 19.1% | 9.0% |

Higher gross margin at scale does NOT translate to higher contribution margin — fixed marketing spend inflates fastest at the $10M–$50M "Messy Middle."

## Investor thresholds (Series A — SCALE framework)

- **LTV:CAC** minimum 3:1 (below 2:1 = structural problem)
- **CAC payback** under 12 months
- **Repeat purchase rate** 25%+ within 12 months
- **Single-channel revenue concentration** under 70%
- **Organic growth** 40%+ of total growth from non-paid sources
- **Contribution margin** minimum 30% (target 50–70%)

## Valuation multiples (2H 2024 M&A market)

- Revenue multiple median: **2.83x**

- Profitable DTC: **1.84x EV/Sales** average

## Contribution margin waterfall (always show this)

```
Revenue
- COGS                                    → CM1 (gross margin)
- Fulfillment + shipping + returns        → CM2
- Variable marketing (paid ads)           → CM3 (contribution margin)
- Fixed costs (ops, G&A, tech)            → EBITDA
```

**Apparel trap:** 60% gross margin commonly collapses to 4% net once 24–30% returns + $20–35/return processing are priced in. Always model CM2 for apparel, never stop at CM1.

## Standard vocabulary (use exactly)

- **Blended CAC** vs **new-customer CAC** — the spread is the retention efficiency signal
- **MER** (Marketing Efficiency Ratio) — total revenue ÷ total marketing spend; board-level metric
- **Incrementality** — true lift after removing would-have-happened-anyway conversions
- **MMM** (Media Mix Modeling) — post-iOS14 standard for >$500K/mo spenders
- **Post-iOS14 / ATT** — Apple's April 2021 attribution framework. Meta-reported ROAS unreliable since.
- **CM1/CM2/CM3** — the contribution margin hierarchy above
- **SDE** (Seller Discretionary Earnings) — valuation basis for sub-$5M revenue brands
- **Hero product concentration** — % of revenue from top SKU; >60% = risk flag
